# ELEVENLABS.IO OFFICIAL OWNERSHIP AGREEMENT

## Article I: Foundational Declaration

**Project:** ElevenLabs.io (the "Project")  
**Effective Date:** January 19, 2026  
**Jurisdiction:** International Jurisdiction, enforceable under principles of international contract law and relevant intellectual property treaties.

This document serves as the definitive and sole authority regarding the ownership, control, and licensing of the Project known as **ElevenLabs.io**. It is executed to establish an unambiguous, legally-enforceable framework for ownership and project control.

## Article III: Ownership and Control Rights

### Section 1: Equal and Undivided Ownership
The Owners hold **direct, equal, and undivided ownership** of the Project in its entirety, including but not limited to:
- All source code, software, binaries, and algorithms
- Intellectual property, patents, trademarks, and branding associated with "ElevenLabs.io"
- Hosting accounts, domain names (including elevenlabs.io), databases, and all related infrastructure
- All administrative and access controls to any platform, repository, or service used by the Project
- Any and all derivative works, modifications, or enhancements

### Section 2: Exclusive Control and Access
The Owners **maintain and obtain all access** and administrative control. This includes:

1. **Access Authority:** No third party shall have any rights to access, modify, license, or control any aspect of the Project unless explicitly granted in writing by **both Owners**.
2. **Delegation of Access:** Any access granted to a third party must be:
   - Granted **directly** by one of the named Owners
   - Explicit, limited in scope, revocable, and documented
   - Subject to immediate revocation by either Owner at any time, without cause
3. **Infrastructure Control:** All servers, domains, cloud services, and hosting infrastructure are exclusively controlled by the Owners.

### Section 3: Financial Rights and Benefits
The Owners exclusively retain all financial rights, including but not limited to:
- All revenue, profits, royalties, and financial benefits derived from the Project
- Rights to any commercial licensing fees
- Ownership of any assets purchased with Project revenue
- Rights to all income from partnerships, sponsorships, or commercial agreements
- Any and all cryptocurrency, tokens, or digital assets generated by the Project

## Article IV: Project Management and Decision Making

### Section 1: Joint Decision Making
All significant decisions regarding the Project require mutual agreement of both Owners, including:
- Major feature additions or architectural changes
- Commercial partnerships or licensing agreements
- Hiring of contractors or employees
- Expenditures above a mutually agreed threshold
- Changes to the public licensing terms

### Section 2: Dispute Resolution
In the event of disagreement between Owners:
1. Discussion and negotiation period of 30 days
2. If unresolved, mediation with a mutually agreed third party
3. Final resolution through binding arbitration under International Chamber of Commerce rules

## Article V: Licensing Framework

### Section 1: Public License Grant
The Project is made available to the public under a custom license based on Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International principles, with critical retained rights as specified in the `LICENSE` file.

### Section 2: Retained Rights
Despite the public license grant, the Owners expressly retain:
- All rights specified in Article III
- The right to modify or revoke the public license for future versions
- The right to offer different licensing terms to specific parties
- All trademark and branding rights

## Article VI: Succession and Transfer

### Section 1: Transfer Restrictions
Ownership interests may not be transferred, sold, or assigned without:
1. Written consent of the other Owner
2. First right of refusal to the other Owner
3. Compliance with all applicable laws and regulations

### Section 2: Inheritance
In the event of death or incapacitation of an Owner:
1. Their ownership interest transfers according to their will or estate plan
2. The successor must agree to be bound by this agreement
3. If no successor is named or willing, ownership transfers to the surviving Owner

## Article VII: Jurisdiction and Enforcement

### Section 1: Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed under internationally recognized principles of contract and intellectual property law.

### Section 2: Enforcement
1. **Forum:** The Owners select International Jurisdiction as the forum for enforcement
2. **Arbitration:** Disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) rules
3. **Language:** All proceedings shall be conducted in English
4. **Costs:** The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to reasonable legal fees

### Section 3: Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

## Article VIII: Amendments

This Agreement may only be amended by a written document signed by both Owners, explicitly referencing this Agreement and the specific changes being made.

## Article IX: Duration

This Agreement remains in effect indefinitely, binding upon the Owners and their respective heirs, successors, and permitted assigns.
